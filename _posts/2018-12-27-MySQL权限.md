## MySQL 权限分配
# 读写权限
````shell
mysql>show grants for wr;
+------------------------------------------------------+
| Grants for wr@%                                 |
+------------------------------------------------------+
| GRANT USAGE ON *.* TO 'diamond'@'%'                  |
| GRANT ALL PRIVILEGES ON `diamond`.* TO 'wr'@'%' |
+------------------------------------------------------+

````
